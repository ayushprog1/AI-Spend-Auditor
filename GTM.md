# Go-to-Market Strategy

**Target User**
We are not targeting "startups" generally. Our exact target user is a **Fractional CFO** or a **VP of Engineering** at a Series A or B startup (50-150 employees). At this stage, companies have scaled their headcount enough that individual software licenses (Cursor, Copilot, ChatGPT) are expensed wildly across different corporate cards, but they haven't yet hired a dedicated procurement officer to audit them. 

**The Trigger**
They want this tool right after their monthly financial reconciliation, specifically when the CFO pings the VP of Engineering asking: *"Why did our OpenAI and Anthropic API bills spike another 30% this month, and why are we paying for 40 Cursor seats if we only have 25 engineers?"*

**Where They Hang Out**
* **Subreddits:** r/SaaS, r/startups, r/devops.
* **Slack/Discord:** Engineering Leadership communities like Rands Leadership Slack, or niche fractional CFO networks.
* **X (Twitter):** Following accounts that discuss SaaS unit economics, runway optimization, and AI infrastructure costs (e.g., Gergely Orosz, Jason Lemkin).

**First 100 Users in 30 Days ($0 Budget)**
1.  **The "Audit Teardown" Thread:** I will run mock audits on popular open-source or public tech stacks and post the teardowns on X and Hacker News. Example hook: *"I ran the math on a standard 50-person engineering team's AI stack. They are burning $42,000/year on redundant Copilot and ChatGPT seats. Here is the free tool to audit your own stack."*
2.  **Fractional CFO Direct Outreach:** Cold DM 50 fractional CFOs on LinkedIn. Pitch: *"I built a free tool that instantly identifies AI software overspend for your portfolio companies. It takes 60 seconds and makes you look like a hero to your founders."*
3.  **Newsletter Placements:** Reach out to writers of mid-tier engineering newsletters (5k-10k subs). Offer them a custom, co-branded version of the audit tool link to share with their audience as a free resource.

**The Unfair Distribution Channel**
The unfair advantage is **Credex's proprietary discount pool**. Any competitor can build a calculator, but only Credex can say: *"You are overspending by $2,000 a month on retail Anthropic APIs. Click here to instantly route your billing through Credex and capture a 20% discount today."* The tool isn't just a calculator; it is an immediate checkout portal for savings.

**Week-1 Traction Metrics**
If successful, Week 1 looks like:
* 2,500 unique page views (driven by HN/X launch).
* 300 audits completed (12% conversion from visitor).
* 45 emails captured (15% of completed audits).
* 5 high-value leads (>$500/mo savings) booking a Credex consultation.
