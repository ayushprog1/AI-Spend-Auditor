# Unit Economics

**The Value of a Converted Lead**
Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits. Assume a startup audits their stack and finds they are spending $5,000/month on Anthropic and OpenAI APIs. 
* If Credex offers a 15% discount, the customer pays Credex $4,250/mo.
* If Credex acquires those credits wholesale at a 30% discount ($3,500/mo), Credex's gross margin is **$750/month per customer**.
* Assuming an average 12-month retention, the Lifetime Value (LTV) of a converted high-savings lead is roughly **$9,000**.

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) by Channel**
Based on the $0 budget GTM plan, our CAC is essentially the cost of my time/labor to distribute the tool. 
* **Hacker News / X Launch:** Time spent writing = 4 hours. Generates ~2 converted leads. Effective CAC: **Nearly $0** (highly scalable but unpredictable).
* **Fractional CFO Outreach:** Time spent sourcing and emailing = 10 hours. Generates ~3 converted leads. Effective labor CAC: **~$150 per lead**.

**The Conversion Funnel to Profitability**
To make this tool a sustainable growth engine, we need to hit these baseline funnel metrics:
1.  **Traffic to Audit:** 15% of landing page visitors complete the form.
2.  **Audit to Email (Lead Capture):** 10% of users who see their results enter their email.
3.  **Email to Consultation:** 20% of captured leads have >$500 in savings and book a call.
4.  **Consultation to Purchase:** 25% of booked calls buy Credex credits.

*Math:* For every 10,000 visitors $\rightarrow$ 1,500 audits $\rightarrow$ 150 leads $\rightarrow$ 30 booked calls $\rightarrow$ 7.5 closed deals.
At an LTV of $9,000, 7.5 deals = **$67,500 in new lifetime revenue per 10,000 visitors**.

**The Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months**
To generate $1,000,000 in Annual Recurring Revenue, we need roughly **111 active customers** generating $750/mo in margin.
To get 111 customers at our 0.075% end-to-end conversion rate (from visitor to closed deal), the tool needs to attract **148,000 visitors over 18 months** (about 8,200 visitors a month). 

To make this true, the tool cannot just be a one-off launch. It must have:
1.  **A Viral Loop:** The "Shareable Public URL" feature must be highly optimized for social proof so every 1 user brings in 1.2 new users.
2.  **SEO Dominance:** The tool must rank on page 1 for search terms like "OpenAI API cost calculator" or "Cursor vs Copilot pricing."
