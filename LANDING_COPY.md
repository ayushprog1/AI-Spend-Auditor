**FAQ (5 Real Q&As)**

**1. How exactly does this tool calculate my savings?**
The audit engine compares your current stack against the official, up-to-date pricing pages of every major AI tool. It looks for seat mismatches (e.g., paying for Team plans with only 1 user), redundant tools (paying for Copilot and Cursor), and retail markup on APIs.

**2. Do I need to connect my AWS billing or corporate credit card?**
No. This is a purely manual input tool. You just tell us what tools you use and how many seats you have. We don't need access to your internal systems.

**3. Why is this tool completely free?**
Credex is an AI infrastructure company. We offer this tool for free because when we find companies that are overspending by thousands of dollars a month on retail APIs, we can usually help them capture those savings by consolidating their billing through our discounted credit pools. 

**4. Is my company's financial data stored publicly?**
No. If you choose to save your report, your email and company details are kept strictly confidential. The "Shareable Link" feature completely anonymizes your report, stripping all personally identifiable information before generating a public link.

**5. How often is the pricing data updated?**
We monitor the pricing pages of OpenAI, Anthropic, GitHub, Cursor, and others weekly to ensure the audit engine's recommendations reflect the most current market rates.
